
### 1. **Work & Office Life**

- **der Fehler** – bug/mistake  
- **die App** – app  
- **die Pause** – break  
- **der Kaffee** – coffee

### 9. **Casual German & Slang**
- **halt / eben / mal / doch / schon** – modal filler words  
- **krass** – crazy / intense  
- **geil** – awesome / cool  
- **naja** – well...  
- **ach so!** – ohh, I see!  
- **quatschen** – to chat  
- **Bock haben (auf)** – to feel like (doing sth)
